Fun Fact, I was born in Bolivia!

My birthday is March 1st 1993. I was born in the South American country of Bolivia, in the city of Santa Cruz.

Fact #2, I was adopted!

I was adopted! My parents flew from NYC to Bolivia in April to come get me and bring me home. I don't know a word of spanish because I grew up in the United States. I grew up in Darien, CT where my father grew up. My adopted parents aren't latino so I never learned spanish.

Growing up in America

This might sound weird but I am forever grateful for growing up in a country like the United States. Was it tough? Yes. Why? Because the town I grew up in is a prodominantly white town. Growing up I was one of a few colored kids attending my school. I was different and I knew it.
